Welcome to ICCIRT 2023

December 7- 8, 2023

International Conference on Computing and Intelligent Reality Technologies (The ICCIRT'23) invites academicians and IndustrialistsÂ in the fields of the Internet of Things, Artificial Intelligence, Augmented/Virtual Reality, Blockchain, Data Analytics, and Optimization, Advanced Computing etc. It provides a forum for international dialogue between various industries, promoting technical developments and spotlighting trending research topics. The amount of data produced by sensors, gadgets, machinery, and infrastructure for the Internet of Things is increasing very rapidly. In order to understand and process IoT data for valuable business insights with higher levels of automation and computing to meet up with the industrial revolution 5.0, there is an ever- increasing need for the use of advanced analytics methodologies, interaction technologies, decentralised trust management, and embedded AI. This fusion of AI, Security, IoT, and interactive technologies will cover the full spectrum from healthcare personalization to the monitoring of massive industrial assets, bringing data out of computers and into the physical world.
We invite researchers, students and industrialists with innovative ideas to submit their research findings.

  • MLOps / AIOps / Devops
